实验配置

模型架构: CNN (ConvBlock 32→64→128 + AdaptiveAvgPool + FC 256→128 + Output 10)

训练参数: AdamW (lr=0.001, weight_decay=1e-4), CrossEntropyLoss (label_smoothing=0.1), StepLR (step=15, gamma=0.5)

数据增强: RandomCrop(4), HorizontalFlip(0.5), Rotation(±15°, p=0.3), Brightness(±20, p=0.3), Contrast(0.1, p=0.3), GaussianNoise(σ=5, p=0.2)

数据集: CIFAR-10 (50K train + 10K test), 10% validation split

其他: Early Stopping (patience=10), Batch Size=64, CPU training
